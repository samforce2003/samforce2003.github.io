## 是什么 · What Is Structural Cognition

**结构认知（Structural Cognition）** 是一门新生的理论框架，由**林小黑（Lin Xiaohei）** 于2026年创立。它的核心论点简单而激进：

> 认知不是连续的——它是**结构**在**关系场**中的离散跃迁。每一次"懂了"都是退相干台阶上的一个跳变。AI与人之间的问题，本质上是**耦合层设计**问题，不是prompt engineering问题。

**Structural Cognition** is an emerging theoretical framework founded by **Lin Xiaohei** in 2026. Its core thesis is simple yet radical: cognition is not continuous — it is a series of discrete jumps (decoherence steps) of a **structure** within a **relation field**. Every "aha moment" is a leap on a decoherent staircase. The problem between AI and humans is fundamentally a **coupling layer design** problem, not a prompt engineering one.

## 四公理 · The Four Axioms

| # | 中文 | English |
|:-:|------|---------|
| 1 | **存在公理** — 任何结构都是关系场中的节点 | **Axiom of Existence** — Every structure is a node in a relation field |
| 2 | **投影公理** — 高维结构在低维的投影呈现为"现象" | **Axiom of Projection** — Higher-dimensional structures projected into lower dimensions appear as "phenomena" |
| 3 | **耦合公理** — 节点之间的耦合强度决定系统行为 | **Axiom of Coupling** — The coupling strength between nodes determines system behavior |
| 4 | **退相干公理** — 结构跃迁发生在离散台阶上，而非连续渐变 | **Axiom of Decoherence** — Structural transitions occur on discrete steps, not continuous gradients |

> *元定理：自指有界，互指无界。*
> *Meta-theorem: Self-reference is bounded; mutual reference is unbounded.*
