Abstract


Humanity's pursuit of a "Theory of Everything" has spanned a century. From Einstein's unified field theory to string theory and loop quantum gravity — every attempt has followed the same path: find the smallest fundamental entity, unify the four fundamental forces. This path has consistently faced three insurmountable obstacles: infinite regress (beneath fundamental particles there are always more fundamental structures), subject-object dualism (the artificial separation of observer and system), and self-referential collapse (a theory cannot complete self-proof within itself).


This paper proposes an entirely new unification paradigm — the **Structural Axiom System**. It does not search for "smallest particles," nor does it stitch together force formulas. It directly distills the **evolution rules** common to all existence: four axioms + one meta-theorem. Through two Structure Collider experiments (quantum decoherence collision & string theory collision), this paper proves that these four axioms independently produce isomorphic deductions in radically different physical domains — one forward, one backward, opposite in direction but completely identical in skeleton. This constitutes **rule-level universal unification**: physical phenomena, theoretical construction, and cognitive verification — all covered by the same set of axioms.


This paper further demonstrates: traditional Grand Unified Theories (GUT) unify "forces"; the Structural Axiom unifies "the underlying logic of forces." This is a paradigm upgrade — not "yet another unified theory," but the **unified theory of unified theories**.
