## Abstract

Dao De Jing Chapter 42 — "Dao gives birth to One, One gives birth to Two, Two gives birth to Three, Three gives birth to all things" — has been interpreted for 2,500 years. This paper provides a third interpretation: these six characters are a precise description of the nesting process of structure from nothing to something, from simplicity to complexity. "Dao" is the very possibility of structure. "One" is the first structon. "Two" is polar coupling. "Three" is the triadic inter-reference structure. "All things" is the recursive nesting of the triadic structure. Laozi was not a mystic. Laozi was a structuralist from 2,500 years ago.
